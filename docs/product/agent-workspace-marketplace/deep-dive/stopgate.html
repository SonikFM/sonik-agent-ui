Framework decision — awaiting ratification

STAY

Vercel AI SDK + Sonik contracts. Do not adopt Mastra as the agent framework; steal its memory-config shape as a contract of our own. Low lock-in — the hybrid option stays open.

Fact 1
Mastra has no approval authority: resume payload is untyped, and approveToolCallGenerate requires only a runId + toolCallId — anyone holding them can approve. Our host-signed reducer refuses to even represent that.
Fact 2
Four breaking renames of run/session/HITL primitives in one month on the stable channel; the Mastra npm package appears in the Mar–Jun 2026 supply-chain compromise wave. Wrong risk under a trust product.
Fact 3
A switch rewrites what we already ship: capability registry, pinning, reducer/controller, and a live host-signed commit path — all Workers-deployed today with zero framework-adapter risk.

Full analysis: docs/research/mastra-vs-ai-sdk-decision-2026-07.md · Source pass correction folded in: their suspend/resume HITL is real; the missing category is verifiable, non-repudiable authorization — which nobody in the surveyed field has.

The finding underneath all of it

Ten codebases in: the field converged on workflow-as-data and one interpreter — and not one of them makes approval verifiable or issues receipts. Every open question above is about how fast and how visibly we ship the category nobody else has.
