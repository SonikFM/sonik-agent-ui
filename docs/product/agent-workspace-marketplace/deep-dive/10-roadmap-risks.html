The two things holding the live path

Everything from persistence outward waits on decisions, but only two items block the work that is otherwise ready to run:

Registry coverage — 8 of 72 commands

Live policy is reproduced exactly through the registry, but live grant pinning is blocked until the registry covers the catalog. Until then the over-grant risk is mitigated in mechanism, not in production.

Controller cutover — Phase 3a-2

The generic interpreter is green over workflowDefinitionSchema, with mutating effects refused outside the commit gate at schema and controller layers. It is not yet the live request path; the cutover is an open, owner-Dan decision.

Invariants that do not move

Roadmap stays outcome- and proof-driven, not a feature list. Every runtime slice ships source-cited doc updates and tests. No live writes before the approval-state and RLS model are resolved. No marketplace UI without install-preview permission display. The visual builder is never the first slice.
